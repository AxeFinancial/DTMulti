The tech line difference

The Tech line does not require investor capital. Pre-sold founding member subscriptions fund the build. Demand is validated before a dollar is spent. Subscribers fund development in exchange for a price-locked founding rate. The incubator becomes self-fulfilling.

Jamie's vision

Tie everything to revenue generation and reward the team for it. Dispatchers are the distribution engine for every other line. Their commissions grow as the ecosystem grows. Once we offer subscriptions to the tech, it becomes easy to build businesses with these tools.

Net profit formula — all lines
Line revenue
- Cost of revenue (direct labor, tools)
= Gross profit
- Dispatcher referral commissions (7.5% of MRR referred)
- Dispatcher upsell bonuses (7.5% of incremental revenue)
- Operating expenses (direct)
- Overhead allocation (10% of revenue to DT)
= Net profit — distributed per split above
The flywheel

Dispatchers grow the core business and earn from it. They refer clients to Finance, Marketing, and Tech at 7.5% recurring and earn an upsell bonus every time they deepen a client engagement. Every action that grows revenue rewards the dispatcher who drove it.
